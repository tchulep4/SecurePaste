SecurePaste

End-to-end encrypted text sharing with customizable expiration and self-destruct capabilities. Your data remains private and secure.

The content will be encrypted locally before being sent to the server.

Expiration

After this period, the paste will be automatically deleted.

Self-Destruct
If enabled, the paste will be deleted after the first view.

Secure Link Generated

Security Information:

The link contains the decryption key in the URL fragment (#), which is never sent to the server. Share this link securely with your intended recipient.

Remember: Once the link expires or self-destructs after viewing, the content cannot be recovered.
